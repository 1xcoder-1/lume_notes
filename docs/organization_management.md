# Organization Management

Lume Notes provides robust organization management tools for teams. Organizations (also referred to as Tenants) allow users to collaborate within a shared workspace with centralized administration.

## Roles and Permissions

Lume Notes uses a Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system with two primary roles:

### 1. **Admin**

Admins have full control over the organization, including:

- **Dashboard Access**: Access to the Organization Management dashboard.
- **Analytics**: Viewing organization-wide statistics (total members, seat count).
- **User Management**: Inviting new members and assigning roles.
- **Global Settings**: Updating the organization name and identity.
- **Permission Toggles**: Controlling what actions regular members can perform globally.

### 2. **Member**

Members are regular users who can:

- **View Directory**: See other team members and their roles.
- **Collaborate**: Create and edit notes (unless restricted by an Admin).
- **Dashboard**: Access a read-only version of the management page to see the team roster.

## Key Features

### **1. Team Roster & Directory**

The management page provides a high-level view of everyone in the organization.

- **Identity**: Displays profile images, names, and email addresses.
- **Real-time Status**: Shows member roles and contribution levels at a glance.

### **2. Inviting New Members**

Admins can easily expand their team:

- **Email Invitation**: Send an invite to any valid email address.
- **Role Assignment**: Choose between `Admin` or `Member` role at the time of invitation.
- **Password Generation**: Lume Notes can auto-generate a secure, 12-character password for the new user, which the admin can copy and share immediately.

### **3. Global Administrative Controls**

Admins can toggle the following permissions for all non-admin members:

- **Allow Members to Edit**: Prevents/allows members from saving changes, moving files, or managing tags.
- **Allow Members to Create**: Prevents/allows members from creating new notes, folders, or importing documents.
- **Allow Members to Share**: Prevents/allows members from generating public sharing links.

## Plan Limits

Lume Notes offers two plans:

- **Free Plan**: Restricted to 3 notes per organization and a limited number of members.
- **Pro Plan**: Unlimited notes and members, with full access to advanced organization settings.

## Navigating to Management

Admins can access these settings by:

1. Clicking the **Organization** icon/link in the workspace sidebar.
2. Selecting **Management** from the organization menu.
